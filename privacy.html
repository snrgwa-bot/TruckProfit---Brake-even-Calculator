Privacy Policy

How we handle your data — spoiler: we respect it.

Last updated: February 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Essential Dev Team ("we," "our," or "us") operates TruckProfit (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

By downloading or using TruckProfit, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

TruckProfit is designed with privacy in mind. The calculator data you enter — including your operating costs, load details, mileage, and financial figures — is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your calculator inputs on our servers.

2.2 Subscription Information

If you purchase a TruckProfit Pro subscription, your payment is processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect, process, or store any payment information such as credit card numbers, billing addresses, or bank account details. Apple handles all subscription billing, renewals, and cancellations.

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

We may collect limited, non-personal information automatically to improve the App experience:

  • Device type and OS version — to ensure compatibility and fix bugs
  • App crash reports — to identify and fix technical issues
  • General usage analytics — such as which features are used most frequently (no personal or financial data is included)

This information is collected in aggregate and cannot be used to identify you personally.

3. How We Use Information

Any information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Improving App performance, stability, and features
  • Diagnosing and resolving technical issues
  • Understanding general usage patterns to guide development priorities

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing to third parties, or profiling.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your calculator data, saved loads, cost settings, and history are stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage mechanisms. This data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device passcode and encryption.

We do not maintain external servers that store your personal or financial data. If you delete the App, all locally stored data is permanently removed from your device.

5. Third-Party Services

TruckProfit may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit — for in-app subscription management and payment processing
  • Apple App Analytics — for anonymized, aggregate usage statistics

These services are governed by Apple's own privacy policies. We encourage you to review Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles your data.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. Period.

We may disclose information only if required by law, such as in response to a valid court order or legal process.

7. Children's Privacy

TruckProfit is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Your Rights

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can:

  • Delete your data — by deleting individual entries within the App or by uninstalling the App entirely
  • Export your data — using the PDF export feature (Pro)
  • Manage subscriptions — through your Apple ID settings

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
